Today I learned something new about [New Relic](http://www.newrelic.com) today that helps  with separating applications in APM.  

 - First click on *show labels*

![Show Labels](https://silvrback.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/b6f7f5dd-068e-42e9-8e69-08fa9b971321/show_large.png)

 - Under your application you will see a ⊕ icon that will allow you to add an existing Category/Label or create a new one in the format of **Category:Label**

![Add Category:Label](https://silvrback.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/6a4ac486-57fd-4f08-b7fb-65ab360be8b2/add%20_large.png)

 - After you set a few labels you can now use them in the search box above all of your applications.

![New Search Options](https://silvrback.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/20d27024-8bc4-495f-af71-14a7e608792f/search_large.png)

 - If you click on one of the items in the search drop down you now can roll up to an environment or all applications tagged with a particular label.  

![Roll Up](https://silvrback.s3.amazonaws.com/uploads/727a272a-84c8-42a2-8f27-6435b760b271/roll%20up_large.png)

Now we have nice organized applications in the APM.
