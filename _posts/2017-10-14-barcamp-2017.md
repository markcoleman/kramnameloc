This is random notes gathered while attending the Harrisburg BarCamp 2017.


#Small Data, Big Results
http://noboundsdigital.com

Problems with big data, example of being marketed to a product/service even when the data is incorrect. (new baby marketing when family lost the child)

Big Data is often dumb data, do you have talent inhouse to crunch all this data.

Small Data can be actionable and easier to understand and handle it effectively. 

Trim down the many data points and focus on the easy parts that actually is helpful (web analytics), this helps you build out digital KPI that is actually useful.

https://www.martinlindstrom.com/small-data/
