Good Morning, today is Christmas!  Looking at our tree it was time to recreate something I saw online where you would perform a long exposure of the tree while adjusting the zoom ring.

**First Attempt**
![Illustration from Merry Christmas (1)]({{ '/assets/blog-images/2015-12-25-merry-christmas/01-image_large.jpeg' | relative_url }})
*this was a bit dark so had to adjust aperture*

With my first attempt I dialed in the camera to f22, a 6 second exposure, and iso 200.  The results were ok but very dark.  After a few more captures I found f8 and a 4 second exposure resulted in captures that looked good.
