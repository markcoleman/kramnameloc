Last week I took some time off of work and wanted to focus on getting a few things done that have been bouncing around in my head.  During this free time I was able to catch up on some blogging [[1]](http://www.kramnameloc.com/the-road-to-gulp), [[2]](http://www.kramnameloc.com/removing-server-side-scripts-gulp-preprocess), [[3]] (http://www.kramnameloc.com/using-gulp-inject).  I realized I enjoy this creative process and want to inject more of that into my daily routine.  

##Creative Log
This brought me to the idea of creating a creative log (clog?) of things I did each day.  These thing(s) could be almost anything but realistically will be...

 - Write blog posts (like what I am doing now)
 - Put together Legos (looking at you [kwik e mart](https://www.google.com/search?client=safari&rls=en&q=kwiky+mart&ie=UTF-8&oe=UTF-8#q=kwik+e+mart))
 - Get the Raspberry Pi working I ordered months ago
 - Draw more
 - Photograph something new
 - Edit/organize the thousands of photos I have
 - Record a video
 - Simply say at the end of the day I did X

##My Goal
Create, do, build, photograph, or record at *least* one new thing per day.
