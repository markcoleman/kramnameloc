I always love to install new operating systems and always update my software to the latest and greatest version.  I do do this a bit cavalier at times but have been lucky (so far).  Since I am a strict mac user doing this was super easy as I was able to spin up a new VM in parallels without any trouble at all.

Since I love guides and pictures here is the install in Pictures!

**Download the ISO**
I signed up for the [Windows Insider](http://windows.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/preview) program and downloaded the ISO.

**Read the knowledge base article**
Parallels has a handy [article](http://kb.parallels.com/en/122745) on how to install Windows 10 inside of parallels for mac.  Once downloaded and extracted choose copied
